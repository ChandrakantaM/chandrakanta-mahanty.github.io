About Me

P Suresh Kumar

Pemmada Suresh Kumar working as Assistant Professor in Aditya Institute of Technology and Management, Tekkali. He has 9 years of teaching experience. He obtained his degree in Computer Science and Engineering from G.M.R Institute of Technology, Andhra Pradesh and Postgraduate degree in Computer Science and Engineering from University College of Engineering, Vizianagaram, Andhra Pradesh. He is pursuing Ph.D. degree in Veer Surender Sai University of Technology, Odisha. His research interests include Soft Computing, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning.
