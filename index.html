About Me

Dr. Chandrakanta Mahanty

I am Dr. Chandrakanta Mahanty, presently working as an assistant professor in Department of CSE, GITAM University, Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. I have completed my Ph.D. in CSE at GIET University. The main contributions of the Ph.D. thesis are published in peer reviewed journals (Two Q1 and Two Q2) and one conference paper. I have completed my M.Tech from College of Engineering & Technology, Bhubaneswar, Odisha. During my M.Tech I got Technical Education Quality Improvement Program (TEQIP) scholarship for a period of 2 years. I have completed my B.Tech from NIT, Bhubaneswar, Odisha. I have already published 11 SCIE journals (8 Q1, 3 Q2), 2 Scopus journals, 4 conferences and 4 book chapters. Currently four articles communicated to reputed journal. I am an associate member of The Institution of Engineers, India. I am also associated with NBA and NAAC accreditation paper works. I also worked as technical coordinator for Skill Odisha 2018, Smart India Hackathon-2020, Toycathon-2021, various national and international conferences and faculty development programs. In addition to it, I have 9+ years of teaching and research experience as assistant professor in CV Raman Global University and GIET University. In June 2023, I received an Appreciation Certificate from the AD Scientific Index in the World Scientist and University Rankings. Moreover, I have participated as a reviewer for more than 25 peer-reviewed journals.
